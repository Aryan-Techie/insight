### `'The Subtle Art of Not Giving a F*ck'` by Mark Manson is a self-help *book that advocates for accepting and embracing negative experiences, focusing on what truly matters, and taking responsibility for our lives.*

---

### ***The book argues that the pursuit of happiness is a mistake and that the key to a good life is accepting negative experiences.***

### ***Manson says people should focus on what struggles they are willing to have in life instead of trying to avoid all difficulties.***

---

<aside>
📝 The key to living a good life

`is not giving a fuck about more things, but rather, giving a fuck only about the things that align with your personal values.` 

</aside>

## ***Key** Crux:*

- 1: Don't Try
    - This chapter introduces the concept of the `"Backwards law"`
        - which states that *The pursuit of positive experiences is itself a negative experience.*
- 2: Happiness is a Problem
    - This chapter discusses the Buddhist concept of dukkha, which is the idea that life is suffering.
- 3: You Are Not Special
    - This chapter argues that
        - *people should not believe that they are special or unique*.
- 4: The Value of Suffering
    - This chapter discusses the idea that
        - *suffering is necessary for growth.*
- 5: You Are Always Choosing
    - This chapter argues that
        - *people are always choosing how to value their experiences.*
- 6: You’re Not Going to Find The One
    - This chapter discusses the idea that
        - *romantic love is not something that people should find but something that they should create.*
- 7: Failure Is the Way Forward
    - This chapter argues that
        - *failure is a necessary part of success.*
- 8: The Importance of Saying No
    - This chapter discusses
        - *the importance of being able to say no to others.*
- 9: The Truth About Your Parents
    - This chapter argues that **
        - *we must not blame our parents for their problems.*
- 10: Finding Your Purpose
    - This chapter discusses the idea that
        - *purpose is not something that people find but something that they create.*
- 11: Death Is Everything
    - This chapter discusses
        - *the importance of confronting death.*

## *Quotes*:

> "The key to a good life is not giving a fuck about more; it's giving a fuck about less, giving a fuck about only what is true and immediate and important." 
This quote challenges the idea that happiness comes from having everything.
> 

---

> "The only way to overcome pain is to first learn how to bear it." This emphasizes the importance of facing difficulties rather than running from them.
> 

---

> "Don't just sit there. Do something. The answers will follow." 
This encourages action and initiative over waiting for perfect clarity.
> 

> "People who feel entitled view every occurrence in their life as either an affirmation of, or a threat to, their own greatness." 
This quote criticizes an inflated sense of self-importance.
> 

---

> "To try to avoid pain is to give too many fucks about pain. If you go around giving a fuck about everything and everyone without conscious thought or choice-well then you're going to get fucked." 
This highlights the futility of trying to avoid all negativity.
>

<aside>
⚖️ **`*The Backwards Law*`**

![[backwards-law.png]]

> The idea that the more you pursue feeling better all the time, 
the less satisfied you become, as pursuing something only reinforces 
the fact that you lack it in the first place.
> 

> The more you desperately want to be rich, the more poor and unworthy you feel, regardless of how much money you actually make. 
The more you desperately want to be sexy and desired, the uglier you come to see yourself, regardless of your actual physical appearance.
> 

> The more you desperately want to be happy and loved, the lonelier and more afraid you become, regardless of those who surround you. The more you want to be spiritually enlightened, the more self-centered and shallow you become in trying to get there.
> 

*→ NEGATIVITY IS ACTUALLY THE PATH TO POSITIVITY!*

</aside>

<aside>
⚔️ THE 3 SUBTLETies

- Subtlety #1:
    
    > Not giving a fuck is not about being indifferent. 
    It just means you’re comfortable with being different. 
    Don’t say fuck it to everything in life, just to the unimportant things.
    > 
- Subtlety #2:
    
    > To not give a fuck about adversity, 
    you must first care about something more important than adversity.
    > 
- Subtlety #3:
    
    > Whether you realize it or not, you are always choosing what to give a fuck about. The key is to gradually prune the things you care about, so that you only give a fuck on the most important of occasions.
    > 
</aside>

<aside>
⚖️ MANSON’s Law of Avoidance

![[avoidance-law.png]]

- *Extensions of Manson’s Law of Avoidance:*
    - ***The more something threatens how you view yourself, 
    the more you will avoid getting around to doing it.***
- *“WE SHOULD DEFINE OURSELVES AS LOOSELY AS POSSIBLE”*
- *Illustration-*
    - If I believe I’m a nice guy, I’ll avoid situations that could potentially contradict that belief. 
    If I believe I’m an awesome cook, I’ll seek out opportunities to prove that to myself over and over again.
        - The belief always takes precedence (*priority of importance*).

> The man who believes 
he knows everything 
learns nothing.
> 

> The more you embrace being uncertain and *not knowing*, 
*the more comfortable you will feel in knowing what you don’t know*.

The more you try to become certain about a *particular issue*, 
*the more uncertain and insecure you will feel.*
> 
</aside>
